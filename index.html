Workshop Preparation Guide
Bryan Olivari · Liberty Business B2B Academy · Jun 24–25
🎯
Your mission at the Summit
You're building the Liberty Business B2B Academy — a modular LMS platform for high-turnover D2D / OTM sales channels in Puerto Rico. The Summit is your opportunity to learn directly from Adobe's product teams, validate your tool stack (Captivate + Learning Manager + NotebookLM), and bring back best practices to accelerate the Academy build.
Wed Jun 24 Conference Day 1 — Key sessions for your work
9:00 – 10:00 AM
[Keynote] Storytelling for business
Why it matters to you

Your D2D training modules need to hook reps fast — they have the highest turnover of any B2B channel. Storytelling frameworks from this keynote can directly shape how you script M02 (Liberty Business Positioning) and M03 (SOHO Segment Deep Dive). Listen for narrative structures you can apply to training content.

Key questions to bring
  • How do you make business positioning feel personal to a field rep knocking on doors?
  • What storytelling techniques work best for reluctant learners in high-turnover environments?
10:00 – 11:15 AM
What's new with Adobe Digital Learning Solutions
Why it matters to you

This is your product roadmap session. You're using Adobe Captivate for SCORM/xAPI packages and targeting a Cornerstone/TalentLMS/Moodle LMS. Watch for: new AI-assisted authoring features, any updates to SCORM 1.2 compatibility, and how Learning Manager integrates with other tools.

Come prepared to ask about
  • SCORM 1.2 vs xAPI — which does Adobe recommend for 2026 deployments?
  • Can Adobe Learning Manager replace a third-party LMS for our use case?
  • AI content generation inside Captivate — how mature is it for Spanish-language content?
  • Integration between NotebookLM (Google) output and Adobe Captivate workflows
11:45 AM – 12:45 PM
What's new in Adobe Captivate: building smarter learning experiences
Why it matters to you — HIGH PRIORITY

Adobe Captivate is your primary authoring tool for the B2B Academy. This session is essential. You're building system simulations (VU Console LCPR, B2B Quick Sales App, Enterprise Sales Console) and branching scenarios for M06. Pay close attention to new simulation and screen-recording features.

Specific things to look for
  • New AI-assisted slide/scenario generation — can it speed up M06 system simulation build?
  • Branching scenario builder improvements for M08 (Activation, BREs & Returns)
  • Quiz/assessment builder for M11 (Final Certification — ≥85% threshold)
  • Spanish-language support and bilingual content workflows
  • Export to SCORM 1.2 and xAPI — any new options or issues to know?
📋 Note: Take screenshots of any Captivate UI features shown — these will be useful for your Ronald Reaño Krays (Trainer) when building module content.
12:30 – 1:30 PM
Invite-Only Lunch — Studion (Customer & Partner Learning Leaders)
Strategic networking opportunity

You're in a room of customer and partner learning leaders. Liberty Business is building exactly this — a partner-facing training infrastructure for B2B sales channels. This is the moment to share what you're building and learn how peers solve the same problems.

Conversation points to have ready
  • "We're building a modular LMS academy for our D2D and OTM sales reps — 11 modules, Captivate + LMS stack."
  • "Biggest challenge: high turnover means we can never assume baseline knowledge. How do you solve for that?"
  • "We use Google NotebookLM for content generation before authoring in Captivate — any peers doing hybrid AI workflows?"
  • Ask about Studion's services — they specialize in customer & partner training, could be a future vendor partner.
1:45 – 2:15 PM
Unlock exponential productivity for instructors with AI-first virtual classrooms
Why it matters to you

Your trainers (Ronald) currently run instructor-led sessions that the Academy aims to replace or supplement. AI-first virtual classroom tools could help bridge the gap during the transition — especially for modules like M10 (Field Best Practices) that benefit from live coaching elements.

2:15 – 3:00 PM
Say it. See it. Publish it — AI in Adobe Learning Manager Content Composer
Why it matters to you — HIGH PRIORITY

Content Composer is the AI authoring tool inside Adobe Learning Manager. If this can replace or complement your NotebookLM → Captivate workflow, it could significantly speed up the Academy build. Evaluate whether it handles the kind of structured, product-knowledge content you need for M04 (Broadband) and M05 (Mobile).

Evaluate against your workflow
  • Can you feed it existing Salesforce Knowledge Articles as source material?
  • Does it support Spanish or bilingual output?
  • How does it compare to NotebookLM for generating training scripts?
3:15 – 4:00 PM
Deliver one-on-one coaching for every learner at scale using AI roleplays
Why it matters to you

D2D reps need to practice objection handling and customer conversations before hitting the field. AI roleplay tools could be a powerful addition to M10 (Field Best Practices) or as a standalone capstone before M11 certification. Look for: how the roleplay scenarios are built, what LMS integration looks like, and whether it works in Spanish.

4:30 – 5:30 PM
[Workshop] Experience Builder — practice building stunning learning experiences live!
Why it matters to you — HANDS-ON

This is the most actionable session of Day 1. You'll be building in the tool live. Come with a use case ready: consider practicing with a simplified version of your M06 system scenario (order entry walkthrough). This gives you a real artifact to bring back and show the team.

Come prepared with
  • A rough outline of one D2D training scenario (e.g., B2B Quick Sales App order flow)
  • 3–4 screenshots of the B2B Quick Sales App or VU Console if possible
  • Your laptop charged and Adobe account logged in

  • How do you version-control SCORM packages that change frequently?

How Adobe is transforming end-to-end learning from creation to delivery with AI
